How do we get our information

As a free independent telecommunication platform, Telco is specialized in collecting, analysing, and evaluating telecom services worldwide.
With the growing demand for telecommunication, telecom service is considered as the most essential service for everyone. And as the market is offering thousands of telecom, it's quite challenging for consumers. Internet and mobile wireless companies’ network are covering millions of kilometers. All these mobile phones, smart devices, plans, and telecom services vary from one company to another and from one product to another. Service quality within the same network may vary from one city to another, even from a neighbourhood to another. To help you find the best service, Telecey provides a unique platform where everyone can share his experience and reviews. The platform captures real-time statistical data as well to assure integrity.
